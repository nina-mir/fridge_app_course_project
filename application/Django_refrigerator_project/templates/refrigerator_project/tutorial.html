Tutorial 3/5

Refrigerators

Owned vs Friends'

When you create a refrigerator you become its sole owner. You have the ability to add friends, rename, and delete the refrigerator.

If you are a friend of a fridge you only have access to the fridge contents.

Whether owner or friend, you can add/delete items items to/from the fridge, modify the fridge's grocery list, and find recipes.

Tutorial 4/5

Groceries

Tracked vs Manual

Tracked items are constantly checked for availability in the refrigerator. If they aren't in the fridge, they're shown as missing so you can buy them. Once the item is added to the refrigerator it is automatically removed from missing items.

Manual items can be added and removed at any time. It's up to you.

Tutorial 5/5

Recipes

You can discover new recipes by selecting items from your current refrigerator.

Discovered recipes can be saved to the fridge for access at any time.
